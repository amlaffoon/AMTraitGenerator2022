    Glossary

    Athletics: physical abilities or strength
    Stealth: moving undetected or remaining concealed
    Acrobatics: spectacular gymnastic feats
    Deception: ability to mislead
    Intimidation: inducing fear or awe
    Persuasion: convince through reasoning or temptation
    Arcana: spellcasting and magical ability
    Lore: knowledge of history, legend, people, and events
    Religion: rituals, prayers, and divine knowledge
    Perception: intuitive understanding; sensory interpretation
    Survival: the skill of sustaining oneself in the wild
    Healing: restore health through medicine or magic
    Nature: knowledge of the natural environment
    Endurance: sustained physical stamina
